## RCA Findings

### Root Cause: NBA UNDER Collapse (Mar 15 retrain)

The NBA UNDER hit rate collapsed from 83.7% (Dec 2025) to 47.1% (Apr 2026) — below break-even.

**Primary cause (HIGH confidence):** Real-world concept drift. PrizePicks lines adjusted mid-season.
Ground-truth UNDER rate:

| Month | Actual UNDER rate | Edge vs break-even |
|---|---|---|
| Nov 2025 | 70.3% | +17.9pp |
| Dec 2025 | 73.3% | +20.9pp |
| Feb 2026 | 51.9% | -0.5pp (breakeven) |
| Apr 2026 | 37.2% | -15.3pp |

The model is NOT broken — it correctly learned the new market regime. The strategy (bet UNDERs) became unprofitable because PP lines caught up to the season.

**Secondary cause (HIGH confidence):** 8,311 contaminated training rows (Jan 18–26 combo props with `actual_value = 0.0`). These produced false UNDER labels at 10.5% of the training window, amplifying the drift signal.

**Direction blend bug:** Confirmed in `game_prediction_engine.py:284` but NOT in the NBA prediction path (V6 uses `production_predictor.py` which is correct). Latent risk only.
